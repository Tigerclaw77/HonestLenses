Vision Source Katy Ranch has the raw ingredients of a strong local recommendation entity: strong site-published reviews, detailed LocalBusiness schema, a clear Katy address, and meaningful specialty claims for dry eye, myopia control, pediatric care, contact lenses, emergency care, and eye disease.

Memorial also publishes an `llms.txt` file that packages services, doctors, location, reviews, and important pages in a format answer engines can digest quickly.
