## Important Validation Note

This report is a visibility and recommendation analysis, not a traditional SEO audit.

Live Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity recommendation outputs were not directly automatable from this environment. Google also returned anti-automation interstitials for direct search attempts. This V0 therefore separates:

- Observed public/local evidence from practice websites, sitemaps, structured data, `llms.txt`, and Bing Maps/local results.
- A 110-query manual validation set to run across Google, Google AI Overview, ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity.
- A modeled recommendation-share proxy based on observed local visibility and AI-readiness signals.

Treat the recommendation-share counts below as a directional proxy until the manual query ledger is completed.

## Executive Summary

Vision Source Katy Ranch has stronger raw entity assets than many local optometry competitors: a high site-published aggregate rating, rich LocalBusiness schema, a clear Katy address, strong service keywords, and specialty claims for dry eye, myopia control, contact lenses, eye disease, and pediatric care.

The practice does not appear to own generic local discovery. In the observed Bing Maps proxy for `Optometrist Katy TX`, competitors such as Today’s Vision Cinco Ranch South, Deja Vu Optometry, Eye Site, Grand Lakes Eye Care, Katy Vision Center, and Brilliant Eye Doctors surfaced while Vision Source Katy Ranch was not visible in the captured first-page generic list. Exact-match entity visibility is strong, but unbranded recommendation visibility is likely only moderate.

The biggest product-validation finding is that the gap is highly actionable. Vision Source Katy Ranch already has service content and structured data, but several important service URLs are missing from the visible sitemap and at least the dry eye page is marked `noindex`. Meanwhile, Memorial Eye Center - Cinco Ranch has a model-friendly `llms.txt`, explicit doctor/service/entity summaries, and a broader specialty page graph. That contrast strongly supports an AI visibility product thesis.

Final verdict: **visibility growth potential is High**. If the target fixes indexability, model-readable entity structure, specialty coverage, doctor profiles, local proof, and recurring AI recommendation tracking, it should be able to beat many independent local competitors and compete seriously with Memorial Eye Center and Today’s Vision for optometry-specialty recommendations. Berkeley Eye Center is harder to beat for surgical and ophthalmology intent.

## Top 5 Opportunities

1. Fix indexability and sitemap coverage for core services.

   The target’s dry eye page contains useful content but is marked `noindex`. This is the clearest high-impact gap. Dry eye, myopia control, specialty contacts, pediatric exams, emergency eye care, and diabetic/glaucoma pages should be crawlable, internally linked, and included in the sitemap.

2. Publish an AI-readable practice entity layer.

   Add `llms.txt` and an AI summary page with the practice name, address, phone, service categories, doctors, insurance/payment basics, review count, areas served, top pages, and citation links. Memorial is already doing this; the target should not concede that advantage.

3. Build specialty recommendation pages, not generic SEO pages.

   Create answer-oriented pages for:

   - Dry eye treatment in Katy / Cinco Ranch
   - Myopia control in Katy
   - MiSight and Ortho-K in Katy
   - Scleral lenses and keratoconus contacts in Katy
   - Contact lens fittings for astigmatism and multifocal needs
   - Emergency eye care in Katy
   - Pediatric eye exams in Katy

4. Strengthen doctor-level authority.

   AI systems often recommend providers, not just businesses. Create doctor profiles with credentials, specialties, quotes, FAQs, conditions treated, photos, and structured data.

5. Launch recurring AI visibility reporting.

   Run the query set monthly across Google AI Overview, ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity. Score mentions, order, citations, and competitor overlap. This turns the product from “SEO work” into a measurable territory-ownership system.
